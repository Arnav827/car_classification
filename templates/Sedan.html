What is a Sedan?

Definition

A sedan is a passenger car in a three-box configuration with separate compartments for engine, passenger, and cargo. Sedans typically have four doors and a traditional trunk.

Who is it Suitable For?

Sedans are ideal for individuals, couples, or small families who value comfort, fuel efficiency, and style. They’re perfect for city driving, commuting, and highway cruising.

Advantages

  • Fuel efficient and aerodynamic
  • Comfortable and quiet ride
  • More affordable than SUVs or trucks
  • Better handling and cornering

Disadvantages

  • Less cargo space than hatchbacks or SUVs
  • Lower ground clearance
  • Not ideal for off-road or rough terrain
  • Rear headroom can be tight in some models
