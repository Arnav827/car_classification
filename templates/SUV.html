What is an SUV?

Definition

An SUV (Sport Utility Vehicle) is a versatile vehicle designed for both on-road and off-road capabilities. It typically features higher ground clearance, spacious interiors, and a more rugged design compared to regular cars.

Who is it Suitable For?

SUVs are ideal for families, adventurers, or anyone who enjoys outdoor activities. They are also perfect for those looking for a spacious vehicle with more power and higher ground clearance.

Advantages

  • Spacious interior
  • Versatility for off-road and city driving
  • Higher driving position for better visibility
  • Improved towing capacity

Disadvantages

  • Higher fuel consumption
  • More expensive compared to sedans
  • Can be difficult to park due to larger size
