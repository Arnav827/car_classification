What is a Van?

Definition

A van is a type of road vehicle used for transporting goods or people. Vans are larger than cars but smaller than trucks or buses, and are known for their interior space and versatility.

Who is it Suitable For?

Vans are ideal for families, group travel, or businesses that require transport for multiple passengers or equipment. Great for shuttle services, large families, and mobile professionals.

Advantages

  • Large interior space for passengers and cargo
  • Comfortable for long-distance travel
  • Sliding doors for easy access
  • Versatile seating and storage configurations

Disadvantages

  • Can be harder to maneuver in tight spaces
  • Lower fuel efficiency than smaller vehicles
  • Not ideal for sporty or solo driving
  • Higher maintenance costs for large models
