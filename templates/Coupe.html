What is a Coupe?

Definition

A coupe is a two-door car with a fixed roof and a sporty appearance. Coupes often feature a sloping rear roofline and prioritize style and performance over passenger or cargo space.

Who is it Suitable For?

Coupes are perfect for individuals or couples who prioritize driving excitement, sleek design, and sporty performance. They’re a top choice for car enthusiasts and weekend drivers.

Advantages

  • Sporty handling and quick acceleration
  • Stylish and sleek design
  • Typically more lightweight
  • Ideal for solo or couple driving

Disadvantages

  • Limited rear-seat space
  • Less cargo capacity
  • Less practical for families
  • Higher insurance costs
