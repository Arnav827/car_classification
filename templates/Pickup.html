What is a Pickup Truck?

Definition

A pickup truck is a light-duty vehicle with an enclosed cab and an open cargo area with low sides and tailgate. It’s built for utility and hauling.

Who is it Suitable For?

Pickup trucks are ideal for people who need to transport tools, equipment, or goods. Great for construction workers, tradespeople, and outdoor adventurers.

Advantages

  • High towing and payload capacity
  • Durable and rugged build
  • All-terrain capability
  • Spacious cabins in crew models

Disadvantages

  • Lower fuel efficiency
  • Harder to park in cities
  • May be overkill for daily commuting
  • Often more expensive than sedans
